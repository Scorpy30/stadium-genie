# Sample Venue FAQ — SoFi Stadium, Inglewood, CA (replace with real venue data)

Q: Where is the nearest accessible entrance to Gate C?
A: The accessible entrance is at the West Plaza, 25 meters west of Gate C,
with a level ramp straight into the main concourse and no stairs.

Q: What time do gates open before kickoff?
A: Gates open 2.5 hours before kickoff. The West Plaza accessible entrance
opens 15 minutes earlier than the general gates to avoid crowding.

Q: Where can I recycle plastic cups?
A: Blue recycling bins are at every main concourse pillar; compost bins are
next to all food concession stands.
