# Sample Venue FAQ (replace with real venue data)

Q: Where is the nearest accessible entrance to Gate C?
A: The accessible entrance is 40 meters north of Gate C, marked with blue
signage, and includes a ramp and a dedicated lift to the upper concourse.

Q: What time do gates open before kickoff?
A: Gates open 3 hours before kickoff. Bag checks are fastest between hour 3
and hour 2 before kickoff.

Q: Where can I recycle plastic cups?
A: Blue bins are located at every concourse intersection; food waste goes in
the green bins next to concession stands.
